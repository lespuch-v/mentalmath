Introduction to Trachtenberg System

The Trachtenberg System is a method of rapid mental calculation developed by Jakow Trachtenberg while he was in a Nazi concentration camp. It provides methods for performing arithmetic operations quickly without using multiplication tables beyond 5 × 5.

Basic Rules

Multiplication by 11

1. Look at the right-most digit

2. Add adjacent digits

3. Look at the left-most digit

Example: 12 × 11
